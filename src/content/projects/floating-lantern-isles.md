## The Brief

The game needed a warm, inviting sky biome — floating islands players could hop between, lit by
lanterns that read clearly from a distance to guide exploration.

## Approach

Each island was blocked out as a modular Blender kit (base shapes, foliage clusters, lantern posts)
so level designers could dress new islands without waiting on an artist. Lighting was tuned in Unreal
with a day/night lantern-glow toggle driven by a single material parameter.

## Result

Level designers built out an entire biome from the kit in under a week, with zero additional art
support needed beyond the initial handoff.
