## The Brief

A guardian creature made of living ice — the silhouette needed to read instantly at a distance while
holding up under close-up crystal detail shots for a cinematic key art pass.

## Approach

Blocked out proportions and silhouette in ZBrush, then pushed hard into crystal fracture detail using
custom alphas and a layered damage pass. Textures were baked and finished in Substance Painter with a
triplanar frost material, then lit and rendered in Marmoset Toolbag for the final presentation pass.

## Result

The crystal shading approach became the basis for a small reusable "frost material" kit reused across
three later creature sculpts.
